Program Description


BreakerBots engineering is responsible for the construction of the robot. This includes the design, choosing all the parts that we are going to buy, cutting anything that needs to be cut and assembling the parts into a working robot. insert pic of engineering team The team typically builds two robots during the build season - one for prototyping and one for competition. The team tries to complete the prototype robot as soon as possible to give the drive and programming teams time to get familiar with the features of the robot.

Skills


  • CAD/CAM insert pic of cad on screen
  • CNC (Tormach 770, CNC router) insert pic of Tormach
  • Metal cutting (horizontal and vertical bandsaws) insert pic of horizontal bandsaw
  • Basic mechanical skills insert pic of people drilling things insert pic of completed robot
  • The engineering team is looking for out of the box thinkers and tinkerers. Many budding engineers come to the engineering team with few skills in metal work - not to worry, the team has taught everyone that’s asked how to build complex systems.
  • The team also spends a lot of time testing the systems - trying to anticipate dynamic loading (what happens when you run into a wall at full speed?), determining optimum gear ratios and the interaction of complex systems, all while keeping total weight in mind.
