Meeting Schedule

Near the beginning of every year, Dean Kamen's FIRST kicks off their worldwide Build-Bot competition. During this approximately 8 week preparation period (January, February, and March), we enter the busiest time of the year and meet more often than usual. Here is our build season schedule. During the off-season, we meet considerably less, but continue to learn and teach.
