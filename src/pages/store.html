Laser-cut Animal Puzzles!

3D wooden puzzles, designed and created by the BreakerBots, allow you to put together pieces to create one of four 3D models. Simply snap the pieces out of the ⅛ inch board and connect them with the pre-cut tabs to create a butterfly ornament, a standing butterfly, a shark, or a fox. All models come with step-by-step instructions showing how to assemble. Glue is not required, but can be used to make the piece more sturdy. All proceeds support Pacific Grove High School's robotics program.


Pixel the Fox - $10

Meet Pixel, the clever fox who lives at the edge of the redwood forest. Pixel earned her name after discovering an old circuit board near the robotics workshop and spending hours figuring out how each component connected. Now she's obsessed with patterns, puzzles, and problem-solving—often rearranging pinecones into geometric designs and teaching the younger foxes about cause and effect. She believes the best adventures come from building things with your paws and your brain working together.

The fox is more challenging, but not difficult to assemble. It has 40 pieces and measures 4.5 inches long and 2 inches tall.
